Custom Buildings
----------------
[✔] Added more options for custom buildings (both default on):
    - Repairable (workers can repair the building)
    - Destroy on reset (/rts-reset destroys the building)
        - /rts-hard-reset will still destroy the building


Bugfixes
--------
[✔] Fix max nbt size issue for custom buildings
    - Nbts can only be 2MB large, but really big structures (1000+ blocks) can exceed that when sent over packets

[✔] Fixed ravager artilleries shooting themselves when at negative Y values

[✔] Fixed custom buildings 'buildable by monsters' option not saving correctly

[✔] Destroyed bridge blocks adjacent to water or lava are replaced by matching source blocks

[✔] Fixed a bug where buildings could be constructed overlapping on a laggy server


Quality of Life

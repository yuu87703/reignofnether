Quality of Life
---------------
[✔] Witch lingering potions no longer collide with mobs
    - This means they will more accurately spread across the ground

[🟡] Vanilla mob spawners loaded with Reign of Nether units now have custom spawn rules:
    - They now spawn mobs at any light level (but won't spawn monster units during the day)
    - Max 3 mobs per spawner, or 1 if they're a castle-tier unit (ghasts, ravagers, wardens)
    - They can spawn without any nearby players, but only if there are no nearby units in a 30 block range
    - Spawn delay is always 30 seconds, instead of a random time between 10-40 seconds
    - Mobs are anchored to their spawns (ie. they will retreat back there if you run away)

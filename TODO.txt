Balancing
---------

[❌] Day cycle times adjusted from: (20, 15, 12, 9, 6) to (14, 12, 10, 8, 6) minutes

[❌] At higher wave survival difficulties, enemy wave populations and beacon build/upgrade costs are increased
    - Beginner: no change
    - Easy: +25% beacon costs, +20% wave populations
    - Medium: +50% beacon costs, +30% wave populations
    - Hard: +75% beacon costs, +40% wave populations
    - Extreme: +100% beacon costs, +50% wave populations

[❌] Every third wave now includes an enemy hero unit with level equal to 2/3rds the wave number
